# Superstore Sales Analysis Dashboard

This dashboard provides an **overview of Superstore sales performance** from 2015 to 2018.  
It is designed to help identify trends, top-performing products, regions, categories, and shipping performance, using **Power BI and Excel**. The data is cleaned and pre-processed through Excel and then visualized through Power BI using DAX measures, graphs and charts.

## Key Metrics
- **Total Sales:** Overall revenue generated across all products.
- **Total Orders:** Total number of orders processed.
- **Average Order Value:** Average revenue per order.

## Visual Insights

### Sales by Year
- Lowest sales in 2016; highest in 2018 (7.2 million), showing an increasing trend.

### Average Shipping Duration by Ship Mode
- Same Day shipping is fastest; slower for other modes.

### Top 10 Products by Sales
- Top product: Canon Image Advance Copier.

### Sales by Region
- West region has the highest sales; South region has the lowest.

### Sales by Segment
- Consumer segment generates the most revenue.

### Total Sales by Category
- Technology is the most sold category.

## Overall Insights
1. Superstore shows consistent growth in total sales, with 2018 as the peak year.
2. Shipping speed varies by mode, affecting customer satisfaction.
3. West region and consumer segment drive the bulk of revenue.
4. Technology products, especially Canon Image Advance Copier, are top performers.
5. Opportunities exist to improve sales in South region and other categories.

## Recommendations
- Focus marketing on top products and high-performing regions.
- Investigate 2016 sales dip to prevent future declines.
- Optimize slower shipping modes to improve customer experience.
- Explore growth opportunities in low-performing regions and categories.
